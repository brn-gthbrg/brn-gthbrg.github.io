Welcome to Killarney Farms Swim Center!

About Killarney Farms Swim Center:

Family membership - Families can enjoy the 25-yard pool, or the children's play pool, as well as our barbecue pits and picnic tables. We also have a diving board for extra fun! All sessions are monitored by certified lifeguards.

Guest Visitors and Parties at KFSC! - Select the 'More Information' link. Consult our manager at 408-247-0321 for all the information you need for membership, parties, swim team, lessons etc.
